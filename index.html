Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trippechalo? +
Trippechalo is a community-driven travel brand curating budget-friendly, expert-led trips across India and beyond. We specialize in group travel experiences where strangers become friends and create lifelong memories together.
How is Trippechalo different from other travel portals? +
Unlike online portals, everything at Trippechalo is fully in-house — no outsourcing, no third-party agents. We design, coordinate and execute every trip ourselves. The result is a consistently premium, smooth experience with real accountability.
How do I book a trip? +
Simply fill out the contact form below, WhatsApp us, or call us directly. Share your destination preferences, dates and budget — and our travel experts will take it from there.
Are community trips safe for solo female travellers? +
Absolutely! We have many solo female travellers in our community. Our trip captains are trained to ensure a safe, welcoming environment for everyone. We also run dedicated Female Only trips for those who prefer that option.
Does the price include flight tickets? +
Generally, the listed prices do not include airfare unless explicitly mentioned. The price covers accommodation, transportation from the meeting point, meals as per the itinerary, and all planned activities.
Can I create a customized trip plan? +
Yes! We love creating bespoke experiences. Reach out via the contact form with your preferred destination, group size, duration and budget, and we'll design a custom itinerary just for you.
